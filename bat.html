BAT — Breakout After Trap

Version: v1.04 • Engine: LWMA lock + EMA confirmation

What it does

BAT looks for a trap pattern followed by a confirmed breakout with LWMA and EMA alignment. It includes a robust management engine (GhostBuster) to handle broker-side closures and scheduled safe SL modifications.

Why traders use it

  • Designed for clean trend entries after false-break traps
  • Strong trade management with scheduled SL changes and retro-scan on load
  • Ideal for H1 trend following

Quick Specs

Default timeframe: H1 • LWMA 50/200 • Pullback EMAs: 20 & 50 • Hard TP optional
